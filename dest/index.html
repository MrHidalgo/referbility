Talent Crowdsourcing

70% of the global workforce (a.k.a. the best candidates) does not apply to job postings. They are called passive talent. Surprisingly, 87% of passive talent is open to new opportunities – but those opportunities have to be brought to them.

Source: LinkedIn (2015)

Even worse : Your best possible candidate is likely working for your competitor. Right now, as you read this. And because they already work, they’re much less likely to apply for a new role. It’s a vicious cycle.

And this is where we come in. We turn the public into your very own recruiters by asking them to refer qualified people they know to the jobs you post. Imagine leaning back while hundreds of people work on searching candidates for you... on a pure success-fee basis!

Building your audience

1

Post your job for free, and we start building the audience for your role.

We begin by only targeting the 70% of the workforce that is passive.

2

We then proceed to seek those who are the most likely to know someone fitting to what you’re looking for.

We start by focusing on the industry...

3

...then seniority, because C-level executives know C-level executives, managers know managers, and so on...
