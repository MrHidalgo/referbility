How it works

We pay you for referring colleagues, friends, and peers to great jobs. Refer anyone you know, as many people as you want, and get the reward for every successful referral!

01

Find jobs to refer to

Visit our job board to see all currently active jobs. Every job has a reward allocated to it - this is what you earn when a candidate you referred is hired.

02

Refer a candidate

Refer by providing us with your candidate's name and email. That's it!

03

Your candidate accepts the referral

We sent your candidate an automated email, and all they need to do is uploading their CV and clicking a button.

04

Track your candidate...and get paid!

You can track the status of each of your referred candidates through your dashboard. When a candidate you referred is hired & retained for 3 months, you will get the position's reward through bank transfer!
